# Web3 Bounty Market Analysis

**What skills do Web3 companies actually pay for?**

I analyzed 384 completed crypto data bounties (2022-2023) to decode the market demand for blockchain analysts and data scientists.

![Analysis Overview](web3_bounty_analysis_charts.png)

## 🎯 Key Findings

- **45.6%** of bounties are intermediate-level (sweet spot for career growth)
- **Dashboard creation** mentioned in 36% of bounties (most in-demand skill)
- **Machine Learning** appears in only 0.8% of bounties (massive opportunity gap)
- **Polygon** dominates at 67% mentions (multi-chain analysis is critical)
- **User behavior analysis** requested in 57% of bounties (more valuable than price predictions)

## 💡 Key Insights

### The ML Opportunity Gap
Only **3 out of 384 bounties** (0.8%) mentioned machine learning. While the market is saturated with SQL analysts creating dashboards, there's virtually zero competition for ML-powered crypto analytics.

**Implication:** Data scientists who can apply ML to blockchain problems have a massive competitive advantage.

### Multi-Chain is Non-Negotiable
67% of bounties mention Polygon, 41% mention Ethereum. Cross-chain analysis isn't a nice-to-have—it's table stakes.

### User Behavior > Price Predictions
57% of bounties request user/address analysis vs basic price charts. Companies care about product metrics, not trading signals.
